From the Earliest Times

Established in 1932, St. Mary's College, Bogawanthalawa, has been a cornerstone of education. With a legacy of academic excellence under the leadership of its first principal, Mr. Kidiyan, the school introduced Ordinary Level classes in 1966 and Advanced Level classes in 1985. Today, St. Mary's College is renowned for its commitment to nurturing holistic development.

Innovative IT Spaces

In our schools, IT labs are equipped with computers, often arranged in rows, into two classes providing students with access to technology. These labs are designed to facilitate computer education, and students learn essential digital skills. The computers are typically connected to the internet, allowing students to explore online resources. It improves the technology knowledge of students

Recreational Zone

Our school usually have outdoor and indoor spaces. The playgrounds often include areas for all games, track events, field events and other popular sports. In other side our school has indoor sports also including chess, table tennis. The emphasis on sports may vary between schools and national levels.

Spiritual Hub

St.mary’s college has a small temple for Hindus based on secular. Like that A special units are available for Christian students and Islam students. In all Friday all the students pray in their religious places in our school. And all cultural events and festivals are celebrating in our school. It reflects the equality of religion in our school.

Classroom Environment

The classroom environment of St.mary’s college typically have a structured setup with students sitting in rows/columns facing the teacher. Chalkboards or whiteboards are common teaching aids. That classrooms environments are not only like a building but also under the tree, sitting areas, small tents likewise structured. Classes include all multi-cultural students.
